About FeedNation

Fighting Hunger,
One Meal at a Time

Founded in 2012, FeedNation Foundation has been on the front lines of the hunger crisis — building bridges between abundance and need. We believe every person deserves the dignity of a warm, nourishing meal.

Community-Centered Approach

We partner directly with local communities, understanding their unique needs and building sustainable food systems from within.

340+ Community Kitchens

Our kitchens are more than places to eat — they're centers of warmth, dignity, and hope for thousands daily.

National Reach, Local Heart

Operating in 82 cities across the country, every program is shaped by local volunteers who know their neighbors best.
